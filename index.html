About us

Five decades of engineering trust

Qifaw is the technology partner of choice for industry-leading organizations worldwide. Since its inception in 1968, Qifaw has upheld the highest standards of innovation, engineering excellence, and customer service.

We've set an aspiration to become the world's largest AI-led technology services company, enabling clients to transform across the full AI stack, from infrastructure to intelligence.

With a highly skilled workforce spread across 56 countries and 194 delivery centers, we've been recognized as a top employer on six continents, building client relationships that have endured for decades.
